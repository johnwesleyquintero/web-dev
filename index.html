The Lore

"Some beasts are born in shadow… others awaken in fire."

Universe Overview

A dark, dystopian world fractured between oppressive powers and forgotten lands. Ancient forces seep through the cracks of reality. Fen, the beast, is an ancient entity bound to Wes’s lineage.

Characters

Wes

  • Role: Protagonist / human vessel
  • Personality: Resilient, strategic, emotionally layered
  • Arc: From restraint to self-acceptance; discovers inner potential through trials

Fen

  • Role: The Beast Within
  • Personality: Primal, unstoppable, instinctive
  • Powers: Superhuman strength, regenerative abilities, heightened perception
  • Visual Cue: Wolf-like, shadowy, ethereal, glowing eyes

Core Concept

Fen is the manifestation of everything Wes suppressed: anger, rebellion, power, and instinct.

  • It sleeps within until circumstances trigger its emergence
  • Awakening represents transformation and liberation
  • Not inherently evil — Fen reflects true self and raw potential

Story Arc: Awakening

Wes is trapped in a world of suppression. Emotional chains manifest physically and mentally. Fen begins to stir, whispering in shadow. The single captures the moment of first emergence, both terrifying and empowering.

Future Expansion

The journey continues with the EP: Fen Awakens and the full album REPPIN DEEP, exploring alternate realms, tyrannical powers, and the evolution of Fen + Wes.

"Fen was always within Wes. The single is the first roar."
